Digital Transformation Excel Analytics Project
This project presents a comprehensive business data analysis built on a real-world digital transformation dataset, focusing on how modern technologies impact revenue, efficiency, and customer engagement across industries and regions. All data cleaning, summarization, and reporting were accomplished using advanced Excel techniques.

Project Highlights
Data Cleaning: Preprocessed and structured a complex industry/business dataset for analysis.
Pivot Table Analysis: Leveraged Excel pivot tables, calculated fields, and filters to answer business-critical questions.
Visualization: Developed a dashboard of insightful charts—including bar, pie, and clustered column charts—for clear storytelling.

Business Insights Provided:
Industries and regions with the highest digital technology impact.
Adoption rates and effectiveness of digital tools (AI, ERP, CRM, IoT, etc).
Links between digital skills training, customer engagement, and business outcomes.
Company size and sector trends for technology adoption and impact.
Year-wise and regional changes in business efficiency/revenue.

Key Excel Skills Demonstrated
Data cleaning and transformation
Advanced pivot table creation and manipulation
Pivot chart development and dashboard presentation
Business data storytelling through visualization
KPI and metric calculation within Excel
